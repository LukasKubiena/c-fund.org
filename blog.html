The Strip Europea: A Cultural Innovation Corridor for Europe

As innovation increasingly centralizes in regions like Silicon Valley and China, Europe needs its own distinctive approach. We propose the Strip Europea: a cultural and technological corridor connected by high-speed rail, linking Vienna, Munich, Brussels, Utrecht, Hamburg, and Berlin. This initiative aims to create a network of creative spaces and institutions that embrace Europe's unique interdisciplinary heritage.
