Präsenzkultur

A new cultural movement focused on presence and meaningful connection.

Präsenzkultur represents our vision for a cultural movement that prioritizes authentic presence over digital distraction. In a world increasingly dominated by virtual interactions and surface-level connections, we believe in the power of genuine, in-person experiences that foster deep understanding and meaningful relationships.

This movement encompasses various forms of artistic expression, from immersive installations to community-driven events, all designed to bring people together in shared moments of presence and awareness.

Through Präsenzkultur, we aim to create spaces where technology serves human connection rather than replacing it, where art becomes a bridge between individuals and communities, and where culture becomes a force for positive social change.

Healing

Art and science working together to promote healing and well-being.

Our Healing initiative explores the intersection of art, science, and therapeutic practice. We believe that creative expression and scientific understanding can work together to promote mental, emotional, and physical well-being.

Through collaborative projects with healthcare professionals, researchers, and artists, we develop innovative approaches to healing that combine evidence-based practices with creative methodologies. This includes everything from art therapy programs to research into the neurological effects of aesthetic experiences.

Healing represents our commitment to using our collective expertise to address some of the most pressing challenges in mental health and well-being, creating tools and experiences that can genuinely help people navigate difficult times and find paths to recovery and growth.
