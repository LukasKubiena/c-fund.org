Origin

C is a new kind of cultural institution and movement of builders, artists, doers and thinkers. We funded c inspired by Vienna's secession in the early 1900s. The founding team of this cultural movement, as you can see in the picture above did xyzzy.

We believe current times call for a similar movement. AGI is shaping up to be a significant technological and cultural paradigm shift and through our work at the cutting edge of science and art, we hope to gain and publish valuable knowledge with the public, global institutions, researchers, organisations and local governments during the ongoing transitional period of building AGI.

We are all builders, artists, thinkers and doers that strive for scientific & artistic greatness in our work. Our founding team hence is already busy working on some of above mentioned issues itself. However, next to that we collectively oversee this found and wish to use it to create physical and digital spaces where collaborative and interdisciplinary craftsmanship, science & art are embraced, explored, as well as practiced.

Part of this work is to connect and engage the public to define together how we want to use and live with this new technology in our individual life's as well as in all the spaces where we meet, such as our communities, work, education, culture and politics. C strives for financial and political independence. — However, we are not a break even business. We are a creative business. If this venture and movement goes bankrupt, it goes bankrupt. Until then we welcome you to join us, no matter as a doner, creative or member of the founding team. Lets get in touch!

Building

C Headquarters - The first floor is our publicly accessible cafe, please feel invited to drop by! Here our creatives converse over lunch or hold discussions in the evening over wine. In the patio behind the coffee place is C's atelier as well as our own show- and presentation room. The other floors are a mix of creative spaces, workshops or meeting rooms.
