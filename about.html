Story

We always felt there is a lot going on in the world. Some 3 years ago however, Leon Cosimo and Lukas, three close friends came together to think about how to best contribute to productive solutions to the issues we saw in society. So together, we birthed the idea to funding a creative collective focussed on using art, crafts, science and culture as a way to create a path to a better future.

We all had a deep passion and care for the arts and creative endeavours from a very young age, being designers actors and entrepreneurs ourself. We saw a huge opportunity for an interdisciplinary effort to emerge in our hometown and one of the cultural capitals of the world - Vienna - to make it possible for us to believe in a positive future again.

Inspired by Vienna's Secession and other cultural movements of the past, we founded C as response to growing societal change, tension, and a silod and fragmented cultural and creative landscape we all feel everyday, be it in social media, the tense geopolitical landscape or the news headlines we read everyday.

Very quickly the name c emerged, a anlehnung an Schiles idea for a kunstfond "K", and based on the shape of the body of fornix - a part of the brain thought to play a major role in human creativity.

Many values we stand to happened to perfectly align with the name.

We believe that art & science working closely together, can be a source of optimism and hope for our future. We want to communicate this case for optimism to the wider public through our and other's work.

We are striving for scientific & artistic excellence throughout this pursuit. To that end, we do not shy away from new technologies, but also won't abandon the rich culture and methods from the past for tasteless platforms and tools.

In our spaces collaborative and interdisciplinary craftsmanship, science & art are embraced, explored, as well as practiced and communicated to the broader public.

We strive for financial and political independence. — However, we are not a break even business. We are a creative institution. If this venture and movement goes bankrupt, it goes bankrupt.

Until then we welcome you to join us, no matter as a doner, creative or member of the founding team. Lets get in touch!

Values

  • For all
    Alltagsprache - We do not want to be intellectualists
  • Excellence vs greatness
    We pursue excellence over mere greatness, focusing on meaningful impact rather than superficial recognition.
  • Working on a positive Zukunftsbild
    The future deserves to be imagined as good. We actively work towards creating and communicating positive visions of what's possible.
  • Interdisciplinary
    No single discipline can bring us to that positive future vision. We need to break individual disciplines' long-lasting isolation.
  • Form follows function follows feeling
    Our approach to design and creation prioritizes emotional resonance alongside functional excellence.
  • Care
    Great craftsman have this theory. They say that whether in service industry, product consumer, business or whatever, you immediately sense carelessness—when something is done without care and just poorly executed. So they say, why then shouldn't we be able to also sense when great care is given into something? The quest shouldn't be about making things beautiful but making them with great care. That makes them beautiful, so even an Excel process automation can be beautiful. This is also the perfect counter to perfectionism: Instead of trying to make something perfect, you just put in all the love and care you can. That is something you can control; perfection is not.
  • Guiding lights
    Our goal as a collective is to help society learn to guide itself in a healthy, neutral grown up way. This means we need to be supportive, not assertive - A healthy, well meaning calm, clear adult voice. A minimal frame that holds space without dominating it. For this we foster a love and care for life and all living things.
