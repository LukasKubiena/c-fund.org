About the Fund

C is a cultural institution and fund of builders, artists, doers and thinkers that aims to support global institutions, researchers, organisations, leaders and local governments during the challenging transitional period of building AGI.

We want to connect and engage the public to define together how we want to use and live with this new technology in our individual life's as well as in all the spaces where we meet, such as our communities, work, education, culture and politics.
